Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kidobum. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our TikTok application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

Since this is an automated video upload tool designed for our own internal content management, we do not actively collect personal data from general users. However, when integrating with TikTok API, we process:

  • Authentication Tokens: Access tokens provided by TikTok to authenticate our application.
  • Account Identifiers: Non-personal identifiers (like OpenID) required to upload content to our designated TikTok account.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively for:

  • Authenticating our application with the TikTok Developer Platform.
  • Uploading and publishing videos to the Kidobum TikTok account.
  • Managing and automating our content workflow.

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement standard security practices to protect the authentication tokens and API keys. All credentials are stored securely in environment variables and are not exposed publicly.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository: Kidobum GitHub.
